    Permission to Dance On Stage was a series of concert performances headlined by South Korean band BTS. Due to COVID-19
    restrictions on gatherings and travel, the series included stadium performances with and without live audiences in
    attendance in lieu of a traditional concert tour; the setlist contained numerous songs from the band's past
    discographies.
    The 12-show run began in Seoul, South Korea on October 24, 2021, and concluded in Las Vegas, Nevada on April 16,
    2022.
